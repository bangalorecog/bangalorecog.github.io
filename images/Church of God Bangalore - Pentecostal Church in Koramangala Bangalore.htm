Church of God, Koramangala

We are God centered, caring community of individuals who are excited about having the opportunity to extend to you the same love, hope and joy we have received from the Lord. As a church we have prayer that is heartfelt, music that is uplifting, preaching that is Christ-centered, and fellowship that is family oriented. In every endeavor at our church we strive to put Christ first and glorify God in all we do.

Pastor
Being born in a Pastors home, ever since his childhood, Pastor Jenson Joy's only desire was to be a servant of the Lord. This heartfelt desire was transformed into a purpose by the frequent prophetical utterances by the servants of God which led him to complete his B.D in 2014.

Currently He is the Pastor of The Church of God Koramangala church and stays at the Parsonage of CGI Koramangala. With a strong commitment for constant prayer and a desire to strengthen the belief of all church members, Pastor ministers for our Lord Jesus Christ.
