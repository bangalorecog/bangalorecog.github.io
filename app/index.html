Church of God, Koramangala

Church of God is a determinedly Christian pentecostal church. It is built upon the person of Jesus Christ, the Son of God. The doctrines and practices of the church are based upon His teachings. It has its foundation of faith and practice in the Scriptures and the vital experience of its constituents is scripturally oriented.
